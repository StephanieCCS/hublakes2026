Honors

Championship awards

Presented each year to swimmers and volunteers who embody the spirit of Hub Lakes swimming.

David Gibson Memorial Award

Given to the championship's outstanding male swimmer. David swam for Lake Hiawatha in the Hub Lakes league from age six and became an outstanding swimmer at the local and national level. He won the O'Connell award in 2005 and was lost in an auto accident later that year. The award has since been re-dedicated in his memory, representing the spirit, enthusiasm, and commitment of a Hub Lakes swimmer.

The O'Connell Award

Presented to the outstanding female swimmer, based on points earned, records broken, and sportsmanship. The winner is chosen by a vote from the coaches of all the lakes.

Claire Murray Award

Voted on and presented to the outstanding female summer swimmer. Claire Murray was a lifelong member of Rock Ridge Lake until her unexpected passing in 2020. She is remembered as a key member of the 35-and-over women's relay team, known for a legendary start.

The La Mothe Award

Dave La Mothe was the founding father of the Lake Parsippany swim team and an outstanding parent volunteer who devoted his time to Lake Parsippany and all of Hub Lakes swimming. Presented to a parent who unselfishly devotes time, talent, and enthusiasm to the sport.

Bob Williams Award

Voted on and presented to the outstanding male and female summer swimmer. Bob Williams was the driving force in Rock Ridge swimming for many years and a promoter of the "summer" swimmer in a league populated with year-round swimmers.
